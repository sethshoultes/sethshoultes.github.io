What this is
The same Seth-clone agent that powers /talk/, framed for builds. Pick a starter above or just speak — the avatar grounds answers in the blog corpus, surfaces post cards when relevant, and points at the matching recipe and skill.
How it works
HeyGen LiveAvatar renders the face. ElevenLabs runs the brain — STT, LLM, TTS, and RAG over 21 sethshoultes.com URLs. A Cloudflare Worker mints the session token; the browser only ever sees a short-lived token. Companion essay: The Avatar Reads First.
What to ask
Specific build questions land best. "Walk me through X" is the right shape. The avatar will name the recipe, suggest the skill to install, and surface the relevant blog posts as cards.
Just want to chat?
Use /talk/ instead — same agent, less build-focused framing.
