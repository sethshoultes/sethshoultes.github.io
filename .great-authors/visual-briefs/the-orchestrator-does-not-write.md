# Visual Brief: The Orchestrator Does Not Write

## Concept
Industrial Minimalism. A study in separation and flow.

## Visual Elements
- A single, clean, vertical line dividing the frame.
- On the left (The Will): A complex, architectural blueprint or a series of precise, geometric diagrams (white lines on a dark charcoal background).
- On the right (The Voice): A single, perfectly rendered ink stroke or a handwritten line of text in a classic serif font (black ink on heavy, cream-colored paper).
- The division is absolute. No overlap.

## Mood
Sterile, intentional, high-contrast. The feeling of a laboratory or a high-end design studio.

## Style
Flat design with subtle textures. Avoid 3D renders or "futuristic" neon. Focus on the contrast between the systemic (left) and the organic (end product on the right).
