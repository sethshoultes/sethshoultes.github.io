# Visual Brief: The Deploy Told You Nothing

## Concept
A study in deceptive symmetry. The "success" marker (green circle) sits on a surface that hides a structural failure (the crack).

## Imagen 4 Prompt
A high-fidelity macro photograph of a floating slab of precision-milled frosted glass against a seamless matte grey void. On the top surface, a glowing, minimalist neon-green circle is perfectly centered. Underneath the glass, the supporting aluminum structure is violently fractured with a sharp, obsidian-black crack. Lighting is a singular overhead surgical spotlight creating a soft gradient of shadow. Materials: frosted glass, anodized aluminum, luminous gas. 8k resolution, industrial design photography, extreme detail, cinematic minimalism, shot on Hasselblad.

## Soul Note
Tension between the surface and the substrate. The green light is the distraction; the crack is the truth.
