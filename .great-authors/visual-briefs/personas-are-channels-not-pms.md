# Visual Brief: Personas Are Channels

## Concept
Hierarchy of agency. Passive conduits (quartz cylinders) versus the authoritative controller (titanium sphere).

## Imagen 4 Prompt
A minimalist industrial composition featuring three identical vertical cylinders of translucent frosted quartz, spaced with perfect geometric precision. Floating above them is a single, heavy sphere of brushed titanium. The sphere is the focal point, casting a sharp, architectural shadow onto the center cylinder. Background is a void of soft, neutral grey. Lighting is a diffused side-light emphasizing the contrast between the ethereal quartz and the dense, weighted metal. 8k resolution, product design photography, Bauhaus aesthetic, extreme precision, shot on Hasselblad.

## Soul Note
The quartz cylinders are passive conduits; the titanium sphere represents the 'Clock' or 'Controller'—the only object with the perceived weight and authority to decide which channel is activated.
